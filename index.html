About Me

Hello! My name is Vipin Kumar and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2020 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes — turns out hacking together custom reblog buttons taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

My journey into web development started with hands-on experience in graphic design, logo creation, and video editing for Instagram and YouTube content. I thrive on turning ideas into pixel-perfect sites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and tools like Figma for responsive designs that captivate and convert. Whether it's crafting custom portfolios, educational platforms, or dynamic landing pages, I focus on clean code, intuitive UX, and performance that loads lightning-fast.

  • React
